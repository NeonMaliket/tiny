# Privacy Policy for Tiny AI

**Last updated:** December 2024

## 1. Introduction

Tiny AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Tiny AI (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:
- Create an account
- Use the AI chat features
- Upload documents
- Record voice messages
- Contact us for support

This information may include:
- Email address
- Username
- Profile information
- Authentication credentials

### 2.2 Usage Data
We automatically collect certain information when you use our App:
- Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
- App usage statistics and analytics
- Crash reports and error logs
- Performance metrics
- IP address and general location data

### 2.3 Chat Data
- Text messages sent to and received from AI
- Voice recordings and transcriptions
- Chat history and conversation metadata
- AI model preferences and settings

### 2.4 Document Data
- Uploaded files and documents (PDF, text files)
- Document metadata (filename, size, upload date)
- Document processing results and analysis

### 2.5 Audio Data
- Voice messages recorded within the App
- Audio files uploaded for AI processing
- Voice recognition and transcription data

### 3.2 Service Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns to improve features
- Debug and fix technical issues
- Optimize app performance
- Develop new features and capabilities

### 3.3 Communication
- Send important updates about the service

### 3.4 Security and Compliance
- Detect and prevent fraudulent activities

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Data Storage
- Your data is stored securely using Supabase cloud infrastructure
- Chat history and user data are encrypted in transit and at rest
- Voice recordings and documents are stored with appropriate security measures
- Data is stored on servers located in secure data centers

### 5.2 Security Measures
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:
- End-to-end encryption for sensitive communications
- Secure authentication protocols
- Regular security audits and monitoring
- Access controls and data minimization principles
- Automatic security updates and patches

### 5.3 Data Retention
- Chat data: Retained until you delete it or close your account
- Voice recordings: Processed and may be stored for service improvement
- Analytics data: Retained for up to 24 months
- Account data: Retained while your account is active
- Crash logs: Retained for debugging purposes for up to 12 months
